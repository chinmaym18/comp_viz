Patent Distribution Across MSAs

Visualizing the number of patents issued in different metropolitan areas for a selected year

Regional Trends

This visualization highlights patent activity across Metropolitan Statistical Areas (MSAs) in the United States for the year 2015. The darker regions on the map represent higher patent counts, while the lighter regions show lower activity. Key observations:

  • Regions such as Silicon Valley, Boston, and Austin are leading with high patent counts.
  • Activity is concentrated in technology and innovation hubs.
  • Smaller MSAs show modest activity, indicating localized innovation clusters.
